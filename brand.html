Brand Guidelines

Driven by Curiosity, Powered by Resilience

Tenacious Raccoon is a personal brand that embodies resilience, adaptability, and creativity. Inspired by the resourcefulness of its namesake, Tenacious Raccoon reflects a journey of overcoming challenges and continuously striving for growth. This brand represents not just my skills in front-end development, but also my commitment to delivering work with dedication, ingenuity, and a personal touch. Through Tenacious Raccoon, I aim to connect with others who value authenticity, perseverance, and a hands-on approach to problem-solving. Whether in design, development, or creative endeavors, Tenacious Raccoon stands as a testament to the power of staying true to oneself and navigating the complexities of life with tenacity.

Theme

The Theme of Tenacious Raccoon reflects the visual aspects of a raccoon to it's more nuanced attributes and representations.

Fonts

  • Tenacious Raccoon Logo: Montserrat
  • Webpage Font-Family: Monospace

Colors

The colors chosen to represent Tenacious Raccoon are not just inspired by the physical appearance of a raccoon but by it's nature and what it represents. Greens and browns were selected to represent nature and grounding, establishing a strong, earthy foundation for the Tenacious Raccoon brand. These colors create a base that is both stable and nurturing, reflecting the natural world and the raccoon's connection to it. Charcoal, representing the fur of the raccoon, was chosen for its versatility and adaptability, mirroring the raccoon's ability to thrive in diverse environments. The background color, a greenish-gray, serves as the canvas for the entire page. This hue is subtle, soft, muted, and calming, providing a peaceful backdrop that allows other elements to stand out without overwhelming the viewer.

Dark brown is used for emphasized areas such as the navigation bar and headings. This color is grounded, bold, and determined, but it maintains a quiet strength, avoiding the need to be overly loud or brash. Finally, rustic orange is chosen to accent key features, adding a pop of color that injects energy and creativity into the design. This warm, vibrant hue contrasts beautifully with the more subdued tones, symbolizing innovation and the spark of creativity that drives the Tenacious Raccoon brand.
